Precision of language doesn’t have to be a lost art. I care passionately about communication and about finding the right words.

Nice to meet you. I’m Kelley.

I’m a writer, a marketer, a teller of stories...above all, a communicator. What can I do for you? Well, if you know what it is you want (marketing strategy, editorial guidance, content for any venue, an invigorated social media presence or corporate voice), I can provide it for you. If you...and you’d be surprised at how common this is...if you don’t know quite what it is you need, let’s brainstorm and I’ll help you conceptualize it.

Things I do.

Marketing Communications

Whether you need support in one small area or you want oversight of your whole comm department, I have the experience. [link »]

Project Management

I excel at project management. Give me a complicated project or problem and I’ll help you break it down and solve/complete it.

Writing

Web content, marketing pieces, even satire…you name it, I’ll write it for you.

Social Media

Need more attention on social media? I can help you post effectively and get more engagement.

Editing (manuscripts, résumés, etc.)

I recently edited this novel; I offer résumés/cover letter review or any other proofing/editing needs on demand.

Story: narrative performance or coaching

I’ve been performing stories since 2012. I also teach group courses and offer individual coaching. See blogs tagged “story;” contact me for curricula.
